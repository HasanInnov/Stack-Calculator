Stack Calculator

About Release Notes

App

Calculates Minecraft Stack

Compatible with Windows

Description:


An easy-to-use Minecraft stack calculator app!
This initial release lets you quickly convert any item count into stacks,
with options for stack sizes of 64, 16, or 1, covering all common
Minecraft items — from blocks to ender pearls and snowballs.


Features:
Simple interface for entering item counts, Calculates full stacks and remaining items,
Supports stack sizes of 64 (blocks, ores), 16 (ender pearls, eggs), and 1 (tools, armor)
Lightweight and fast — no internet required Ideal for builders, redstoners,
and survival players who want quick, accurate inventory planning.
